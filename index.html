Yun-Chen Lo  

I am currently at National Tsing Hua University, advised by Prof. Ren-Shuo Liu. I have general research interests in VLSI design and EDA, with a focus on designing efficient AI systems.

I am the first author of several top/major conference papers, including DAC, ICLR, ICCAD, ESSCIRC, and ASP-DAC. I am also the co-author of several top conference papers, including ISSCC and IEDM. In addition, I have three TSMC tap-out experiences.

I received my B.S. and M.S. in Electrical Engineering from National Tsing Hua University. I was fortunate to work with Prof. Chi-Chun Lee (Jeremy) and Prof. Tsung-Yi Ho.
