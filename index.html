Yun-Chen Lo  

I am currently at National Tsing Hua University, advised by Prof. Ren-Shuo Liu. I have general research interests in VLSI design and EDA, with a focus on designing efficient systems for AI.

I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from National Tsing Hua University. I was fortunate to work with Prof. Chi-Chun Lee (Jeremy) and Prof. Tsung-Yi Ho.
